Why Serve?

Every gift has a place in the house of God

Ministry is more than a role. It is a joyful opportunity to worship, encourage others, grow in faith, and use your God-given gifts for His purpose.

1 Discover the ministry that fits your gifts.
2 Connect with leaders and ministry members.

How To Join

Start serving in three simple steps

We made the process simple so every member can discover where they fit, connect with ministry leaders, and begin serving with confidence.

01

Discover

Explore the ministries and find the one that matches your gifts, passion, and availability.

02

Talk with a ministry leader, ask questions, and learn how the team serves together.

03

Serve

Join training, attend gatherings, and begin serving God with joy and purpose.
