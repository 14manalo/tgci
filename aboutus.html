Who We Are

A Church Built on Faith and Community

The Three Are One God Church is a Christ-centered community devoted to loving God, loving people, and serving others. Founded with a vision to spread the Gospel and build vibrant, faith-filled communities, our church brings together believers of all ages to worship, grow in discipleship, and impact society through outreach, service, and spiritual guidance.

Our Story

Church History Timeline

From Bible study cell groups to a growing church community, here is a simple look at our journey.

1985

Bible Study Cell Groups Began

The church's roots began in Damam, Saudi Arabia, where Ptr. Virgilio Medina formed Bible study cell groups.

1991

Official SEC Registration

After returning to the Philippines, the fellowship was formed and officially registered with the SEC on April 12, 1991.

2013

Ministry Continued to Grow

The remaining members formed The Three In One God International Ministries Inc. on February 14, 2013.

Today

The Three Are One God Church

The name was later amended to The Three Are One God Church International Inc., holding worship services across various parts of Luzon.
