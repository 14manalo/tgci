EmailJS Production Inbox Setup Guide

To deliver real-time messages and prayer requests from visitors directly into your actual email inbox, follow these simple steps to hook up your own free EmailJS keys.

1 Create Free EmailJS Account

Sign up at emailjs.com. Add a Personal Email Service (Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo) connected to your church email inbox.

2 Configure Templates

Create Email Templates matching public page forms. Design your subject/body and capture fields: {{from_name}}, {{from_email}}, {{from_phone}}, {{category}}, {{description}}.

3 Insert Keys into Code

Open contact.html and prayer.html. Find the emailjs.send(...) blocks at the bottom and replace the template ID, service ID, and public key placeholders with your live credentials!
